Life Of Pi (2001)


"The Life of Pi" is a 2001 novel by Yann Martel, which tells the story of a young Indian boy named Pi who is shipwrecked and left stranded on a lifeboat in the Pacific Ocean with a Bengal tiger as his only companion. The book explores themes of survival, faith, and the human-animal relationship.


Genres : Survival, Adventure, Magical realism, Philosophical fiction

Fight Club (1996)


"Fight Club" is a 1996 novel by Chuck Palahniuk. It follows the experiences of an unnamed protagonist struggling with insomnia. Inspired by his doctor's exasperated remark that insomnia is not suffering, the protagonist finds relief by impersonating a seriously ill person in several support groups.


Genres : Psychological thriller, Satire, Black comedy, Postmodern fiction


Price : ₹149
